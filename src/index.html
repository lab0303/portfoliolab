GameShop Pro

I've developed a captivating e-commerce website using React in conjunction with Firebase. This dynamic platform boasts a diverse array of video games for users to explore, each offering comprehensive details that include the game's name, price, and a compelling description. As users delve into the specifics of each game, they have the option to interactively modify their desired quantity, incrementing or decrementing their selections before effortlessly adding them to their virtual shopping cart. Once within the cart view, customers have the freedom to manage their selections with ease. They can opt to remove items from their cart or, if their gaming appetite remains unsated, they can augment their selections. The cart view not only displays the total cost of their chosen games but also offers a streamlined process for order finalization. Users can proceed to checkout with the click of a button, where they are presented with a convenient form to input their information for order tracking.
